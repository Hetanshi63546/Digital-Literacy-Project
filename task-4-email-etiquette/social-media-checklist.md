# Social Media Do's and Don'ts for College Students

##  Do’s

- Maintain a professional profile with accurate details, especially on academic and career platforms  
- Share useful, educational, or skill-based content that reflects your interests and knowledge  
- Respect others’ opinions and communicate politely in discussions and comments  
- Verify facts before sharing any news, posts, or forwarded messages  
- Use social media for learning, networking, and building meaningful connections  


##  Don’ts

- Don’t post offensive, abusive, or inappropriate content that can harm your reputation  
- Don’t share personal or sensitive information like phone numbers, addresses, or passwords  
- Don’t engage in unnecessary arguments, trolling, or negative interactions online  
- Don’t spread misinformation, rumors, or unverified content  
- Don’t copy, plagiarize, or take credit for someone else’s work
